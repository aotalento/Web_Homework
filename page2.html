Temperature vs Latitude

For this visualization we use matplotlib to quickly create a scatter plot based on the data given. Where each point is plotted first by the Lattitude and then by the temp at the time the data was pulled from the API.

Obviously as was expected the temps trend warmer as the location gets closer to the equator. However, the highest temps seem to occur slightly north of the actual equator. I suspect factors such as heat waves or time of day when the data was taken as the reason for this.
