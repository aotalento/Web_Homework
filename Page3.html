Lattitude Vs Humidity

For this visualization we use matplotlib to quickly create a scatter plot based on the data given. Where each point is plotted first by the Lattitude and then by the humidity at the time the data was pulled from the API.

In this example while the data seems to be fairly random, which again may be due to the weather patterns in the regions for that day. We do see that interestingly not a single city on the equator has less than about 40% humidity.
